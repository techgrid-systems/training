Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PA Certification Path?

The PA (Power Automate) certification path consists of three levels: PA 1 (Foundational), PA 2 (Intermediate), and PA 3 (Enterprise). Each level builds upon the previous one to provide a comprehensive skill set.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required for the PA 1 course. However, PA 2 and PA 3 courses have prerequisites that require successful completion of the previous certification level exams.

Are resources downloadable?

Yes, we provide official source documentation and automation templates within our Downloads section for all active training modules.

How do I get technical support?

If you encounter issues, please visit our Support page to browse our FAQs or submit a ticket for personalized assistance from our automation architects.
