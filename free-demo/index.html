Module 1.2: Triggering Your First Flow

Foundational Automation • 15 min read

Progress: 25%

Objective

In this lesson, you will learn how to trigger a Power Automate flow using a Microsoft Form submission. We will focus on mapping form fields to your automated workflow actions.

"Automation is not just about replacing tasks; it is about creating consistency and reliability in your workflows."

Step-by-Step Implementation

1

Open your Microsoft Forms instance and ensure your trigger fields are correctly mapped as JSON objects.

2

In Power Automate, create a new Cloud Flow using the "When a new response is submitted" trigger.

3

(Locked) Configure the mapping to your target automation destination.
